CT001

Automated Watering System For Plants

This product is an eco-friendly, automated watering system designed to maintain optimal soil moisture levels for plants. It uses a soil moisture sensor to monitor the moisture content in the soil in real-time. When the moisture drops below a set threshold, a microcontroller activates a water pump to irrigate the plant. The system is powered by a solar panel, making it sustainable and independent of external power sources.
